Meet the Fan Favorite Characters

Jujutsu Kaisen features a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique abilities and personalities. Some of the main characters include Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, Nobara Kugisaki, Satoru Gojo, and Sukuna, the powerful curse that resides within Yuji.

Megumi Fushiguro

#Potential Man

A skilled jujutsu sorcerer and Yuji's classmate, known for his serious demeanor.

Yuji Itadori

#Straight Hands

The main protagonist who becomes a jujutsu sorcerer after ingesting a cursed object.

Nobara Kugisaki

#Absolute Hammer

A confident and strong-willed jujutsu sorcerer who fights alongside Yuji and Megumi.
