Stay on Edge With Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of jujutsu sorcery after consuming a powerful cursed object.

Meet the Characters

Jujutsu Kaisen features a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique abilities and personalities. Some of the main characters include Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, Nobara Kugisaki, Satoru Gojo, and Sukuna, the powerful curse that resides within Yuji.

Megumi Fushiguro

A skilled jujutsu sorcerer and Yuji's classmate, known for his serious demeanor.

Yuji Itadori

The main protagonist who becomes a jujutsu sorcerer after ingesting a cursed object.

Nobara Kugisaki

A confident and strong-willed jujutsu sorcerer who fights alongside Yuji and Megumi.

Get the Manga

The Jujutsu Kaisen manga is serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump and has been collected into multiple tankobon volumes. The manga offers a deeper exploration of the story and characters, making it a must-read for fans of the series.
